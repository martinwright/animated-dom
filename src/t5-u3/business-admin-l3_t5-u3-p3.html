Managing challenging conversations

Whatever the cause of the problem, the main aims when dealing with challenging conversations are to:

  • diffuse emotions
  • identify the problem
  • agree and implement solutions
  • take appropriate follow-up action.

In this section, we will look at:

  • how to prepare for challenging conversations
  • techniques that can be used to manage challenging conversations
  • delivering constructive feedback and criticism
  • when problems need to be escalated.
