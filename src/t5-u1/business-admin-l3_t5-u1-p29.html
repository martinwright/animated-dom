Effective teamwork

15 Show appreciation to the people or team for the work they have done

Recognition for the work done is clearly important. Where people have been willing to put the effort in and develop or respond to a challenge, then recognition of this during the task and at the end is not only right but also part of encouraging more willingness to take on delegated tasks in the future. The task, situation and the amount of work done may have a bearing on the level or size of the reward. What is important is that the appropriate level of recognition and reward is applied, whether it is a heartfelt ‘thank you’ or a more tangible reward of some kind.
