Effective teamwork

1 Challenges and barriers

Despite the benefits appearing to be wholly desirable to any manager, delegation has its challenges and barriers. There is the potential for delegation not to be used effectively or as often as it could. These barriers include:

  • lack of confidence of the manager in themselves and/or their team
  • ‘it's quicker if I do it myself’ mentality
  • time and operational pressures
  • lack of clarity over the work to be done
  • lack of trust within the team
  • low experience or ability levels in a team
  • employees reluctant to take on tasks due to previous poor experiences.
