Effective teamwork

8 The steps to effective delegation

To create the best possible opportunity for successful delegation there are a number of actions we can take. These include:

  • identifying suitable tasks that have the potential for delegation
  • identifying potential team members or teams suitable for the task
  • setting clear goals, objectives and levels of responsibility
  • providing appropriate levels of support, including reviews
  • showing appreciation to the people or team for the work they have done.
