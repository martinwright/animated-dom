Producing business documents

3 Letters

These will be on headed notepaper and will be used in formal situations, especially if a permanent record is needed. They are particularly useful when information is confidential and sensitive and needs to be kept private, for example:

  • during disciplinary procedures
  • when making a job offer
  • when writing to customers, staff or other stakeholders about serious, confidential or sensitive matters.

Formal letters reflect the organisation at its most professional and serious, so good grammar, vocabulary and presentation are essential.
