2 Time management

Time management is the act or process of planning and exercising conscious control over the amount of time spent on specific activities, especially to increase effectiveness, efficiency or productivity.

A time-management system is a designed combination of processes, tools, techniques, and methods. Time management is usually a necessity in any project development as it determines the project completion time and scope.

Time management should help support:

  • creating an environment conducive to effectiveness
  • setting priorities
  • carrying out activity around those priorities
  • reduction of time spent on non-priorities
  • incentives to modify behaviour to ensure compliance with time-related deadlines.
