Producing business documents

4 Reports

Reports can be formal or informal. An informal report can be, for instance, a completed paper or electronic form that is used for:

  • giving daily figures for sales or production
  • reporting costs and other measurable statistics
  • handover notes.

Formal reports can be printed or sent electronically and will cover subjects such as, for example:

  • annual accounts and accountants’ reports
  • directors’ reports for shareholders
  • research and development – eg test results for new equipment; detailed comparison between two production methods
  • feedback and analysis prepared after an event or project – eg to show achievements, problems and opportunities for improvement
  • staff appraisals
  • analysis, strategies and plans on how to achieve objectives.
