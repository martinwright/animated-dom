Producing business documents

1 Memorandums (memos)

In business, a memo is typically used for internal communication, as opposed to letters which are typically for external communication.

Some document software comes with templates which you can use to create memos or you may have a company or organisational style.

Memos are used to inform the reader about new information like a new company policy or price increases, or persuade the reader to take an action, such as attending a training session. Regardless of the specific goal, memos are most effective when they connect the purpose of the writer with the interests and needs of the reader.

When creating a memo:

  • choose who you send it to wisely – only send to those people who need the information it contains, not the whole office
  • ensure that it does not contain any sensitive information – use face-to-face meetings or a phone call instead.

Memos are most effectively used when sent to a small to moderate number of people to communicate company or job objectives.
