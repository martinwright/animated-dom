Producing business documents

6 Forms

Most organisations design and use their own forms. The idea is to simplify the process of sharing information so that the person completing the form can do so as easily as possible. The organisation can target the information that they need and want, so that time and resources are not wasted on processing unnecessary answers and data.

Forms can be used for many functions, including, for example:

  • timesheets, mileage and expenses claims from staff
  • requests – eg budgets, maintenance work, for leave and other time off, or new resources
  • surveys and feedback comments from customers
  • job applications
  • ideas and suggestions from staff
  • orders and sales
  • finance applications – eg when asking for a loan or mortgage.
