3 Working to deadlines

It is important to recognise what work is 'urgent' and that which is 'important' so that you can prioritise. A way to prioritise and complete work that is urgent is to organise it depending on the urgency of the work. It could be a good idea to set up a file and organise the order in which you will complete the work. Prioritising work correctly can and will have a direct impact on other people's work and the organisation, so you need to ensure that you plan time carefully to ensure deadlines and expectations are met.

Most organisations work within specific timeframes to complete work; this is referred to as a service level agreement (SLA). Work has to be completed to meet a set deadline or the work will go out of service and become an urgent task. Some work may incur fines if SLAs are not met.
