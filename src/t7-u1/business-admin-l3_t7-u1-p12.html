Managing resources

1 Purposes of controlling stock

Resource management requires that every resource is controlled. The purposes of controlling stock include:

  • preventing theft
  • preventing misappropriation
  • preventing damage or spoilage
  • ensuring best use of resources available
  • ensuring there are sufficient resources for work activity
  • identifying improvements to systems and use of resources
  • ensuring equipment is used and maintained so as to prolong use
  • identifying more cost-effective ways of working and saving the organisation money.
