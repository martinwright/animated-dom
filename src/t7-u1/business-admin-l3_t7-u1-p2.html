Managing resources

Key terms

  • Resources
    the human, financial and physical factors that provide an organisation with the means to carry out its procedures.
  • Procurement
    the act of obtaining or buying goods and services.
  • Consumables
    products that need to be replaced after they have been used for a period of time.
  • Non-consumables
    items having a useful life greater than one year, or those that cannot be used up or depleted.
