Communicating effectively

8 Spreadsheets and databases

Spreadsheets are used to bring numerical data together – eg to analyse expense claims for all employees under different headings, such as travel, subsistence, mileage and hotels.

Databases are used when names, addresses and other personal details and preferences need to be collected and used – eg for mailing lists.

Both systems are flexible and can be amended and updated by people who have authorised access to them.
