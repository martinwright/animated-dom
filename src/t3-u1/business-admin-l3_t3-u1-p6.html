Communicating effectively

3 Forms of written communication

In written communication we need to think about reading, writing, pictures, symbols and other visual communication.

There are many instances when we need to use written information in the workplace – eg in notes, reports and emails. Written communication can be very important as it is often kept as evidence to show that policies and procedures have been followed.

Written communication can also include pictures, symbols and other visual communications – eg a scribbled drawing to explain something, or Braille versions of leaflets.

Over the next few pages we'll cover examples of methods of written communication in the workplace.
