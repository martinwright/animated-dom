1 Mission and vision

Organisations often use mission and vision statements to communicate their purpose, goals and values to their employees and customers.

Mission statements define the organisation's overall business, its objectives and the approach it plans to use to reach those objectives.

Vision statements describe where the organisation aims to be in the future.

Mission and vision statements can often be combined to provide an overview of the organisation's purposes, goals and values.
