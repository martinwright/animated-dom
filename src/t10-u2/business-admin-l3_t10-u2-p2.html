Key terms

  • Purpose — the reason for which something exists.
  • Activities — actions undertaken by organisations, such as buying, selling, marketing or investing, for the purpose of generating profits or developing economic opportunities.
  • Aims — where the business wants to go in the future, its goals, a statement of resolve.
  • Values — the operating philosophies or principles that guide an organisation's conduct and its relationship with customers, partners and shareholders.
  • Vision for the future — where the organisation wants to go rather than its current state.
