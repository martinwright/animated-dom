Organisational culture and strategy

Key terms

  • Political environment
    government actions which affect the operations of an organisation. These actions may be on a local, regional, national or international level.
  • Economic environment
    the totality of economic factors, such as employment, income, inflation, interest rates, productivity and wealth,that influence the buying behaviour of consumers and institutions.
  • Market
    a system of institutions, rules and procedures relating to the exchange of goods and services between persons or organisations.
  • International market
    a market outside the national borders of an organisation's country.
  • Global market
    the activity of buying or selling goods and services in all the countries of the world.
