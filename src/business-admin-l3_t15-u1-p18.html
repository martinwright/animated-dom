IT packages and systems

8 Security

The majority of organisations store their records and files, as well as other information, electronically. The advantages are huge in terms of accessibility, speed and ease of retrieval and updating, and in saving space. It is therefore important that files held are secure from loss or damage.

In many cases, the information only exists electronically (there would be little point in printing an entire database of customer records) and it is therefore important that the data files held are secure from loss or damage. Electronic documents can be password protected, which adds another layer of security. Making regular back-ups is also recommended.
