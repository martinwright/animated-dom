Organising meetings and events

4 Other types of events

Product launches

This will make the public aware of a new product; such as a car manufacturer's latest model or a new book.

Forums and advice sessions

This is where the focus is on the exchange of information and expertise on a specific subject. These are usually free, in an informal setting and are an opportunity to exchange information. A forum or advice session will have a specific topic and will require a leader/facilitator.
