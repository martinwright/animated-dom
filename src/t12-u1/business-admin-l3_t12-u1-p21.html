Organising meetings and events

3 Training and academic conferences and seminars

These events can be used to train both internal and external customers on a set of learning objectives. They:

  • can be held in-house or at external venues
  • can be over one or a multiple of days.

Team-building events

There is a wide range of events used to develop teams. These events are:

  • used to create more effective working environments for employees
  • generally held away from the workplace
  • held in an informal environment
  • can involve both indoor and outdoor activities.

Why might businesses use a team-building event? Search the internet and research the wide range of team-building events on offer.

Awards ceremonies

These are used to celebrate success or achievement; examples include celebrating the success of learners (eg a college or university), industry awards (eg the Oscars), or recognition of bravery (eg the Pride of Britain awards). Awards ceremonies:

  • are delivered in a formal environment
  • can also incorporate other events, such as dinners and after-show activities.
