Organising meetings and events

Key terms

  • Verbatim minutes
    a record of every single word said at a meeting.
  • Narrative minutes
    minutes taken during a business meeting that record the discussions that take place in detail but not verbatim.
  • Action log
    a record of actions agreed, detailing who has agreed to the action, what exactly they have agreed to do and by when.
  • Logistics
    the management of the details of an operation.
