Organising meetings and events

5 Leading meetings

To prepare well to lead a meeting you need to:

  • have a clear purpose for the meeting
  • prepare an agenda
  • send out the agenda at least two days before the meeting
  • invite only relevant people.

Watch this video to learn more about preparing to lead meetings.

https://youtu.be/-rsUq8CQrR0

During the meeting it is important to remain on task and manage the meeting following the agenda. It is important to encourage quiet reflective people and those who are new to meetings to participate.

Watch the video to learn more.

https://youtu.be/v8dzo2V1KYM

The three activities that should follow a meeting:

  1. Preparing and distributing the meeting minute notes.
  2. Action checks.
  3. Evaluate how effective your meeting was.
