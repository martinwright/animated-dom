Organising meetings and events

1 Introduction

Before an event you should create an event plan, this will help identify what resources are required. An event plan will specify the resources needed and will help with the planning process. Resources identified can be:

  • presenters/speakers
  • staff
  • equipment
  • documentation
  • furniture
  • decorations
  • facilities
  • stationery.

You may also need to consider:

  • the fees/cost of the event
  • catering to be provided
  • accommodation available
  • the presenters and/or the speakers
  • health, safety and emergency procedures
  • the likely types of information needed from delegates.

Prior to the event you should send out invites detailing:

  • objectives of the event
  • venue details, location and facilities
  • joining instructions
  • activities scheduled
  • a map and travel information
  • further information/contact details.

During the event you should ensure delegates are aware of the following:

  • what to do in emergencies
  • location of facilities
  • schedule of events.

After the event you should ensure delegates are aware of the following:

  • details of the next steps
  • where to get further information/contact details
  • how to give feedback.
