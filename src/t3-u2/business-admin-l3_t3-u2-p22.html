Interpersonal skills

2 Beginning of the call

Greeting

First impression counts!

All companies will have a standard greeting, which is often scripted.

This will ensure that the caller knows they are through to the correct department, and that they have the name of the person they are speaking to.

Identification/verification of caller

There are different ways to identify/verify the caller.

Some methods are:

  • confirming personal/account details you have on record
  • passwords
  • memorable words
  • security key.

This is to adhere to data protection legislation and security.
