Interpersonal skills

5 Summary

Ending/closing the call

Lasting impressions count!

  • Summarise the call to the caller.
  • Confirm the caller has completed all enquiries.
  • Confirm any actions to be taken.

System notes should reflect the call:

  • clear and concise
  • correct spelling and grammar
  • full words
  • avoid slang
  • limit jargon
  • dated.
