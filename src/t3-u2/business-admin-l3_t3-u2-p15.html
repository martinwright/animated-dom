1 Different levels of listening

Hawkins and Smith (2006), Starr (2011) and others talk about the importance of different levels of listening. When using deeper listening skills this allows us to not only hear the words but grasp the real meaning behind them, giving us a deeper understanding of the meaning behind what is being said.

These different levels reflect the various motivations and reasons we have for listening at different times. They all focus on ultimately going beyond surface level listening to active, empathetic or deeper listening.

This is not to say that listening on a conversational level is bad, a lot of our general communications work fine at this level. However it may be less helpful when we want to understand someone better or try to understand the underlying motivations behind their words.
