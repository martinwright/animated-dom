9 The phonetic alphabet

What is the phonetic alphabet and why do we use it?

The phonetic alphabet is a list of words used to identify letters in messages transmitted by telephone or radio. Spoken words from an approved list are substituted for letters. For example, the word 'Navy' would be 'November, Alpha, Victor, Yankee' when spelt with the phonetic alphabet.
