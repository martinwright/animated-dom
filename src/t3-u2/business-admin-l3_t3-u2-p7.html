Interpersonal skills

4 Posture

  • Standing straight makes us feel and appear confident.
  • Drooping our shoulders or looking at the ground conveys nervousness or disinterest.
  • Crossed arms can convey defensiveness and imply that you are unapproachable.
  • Hands on hips can makes us look stern.
  • Hands in pockets makes us look nervous especially if you have coins in them.
  • Standing very close to a customer can be intrusive and forceful.
