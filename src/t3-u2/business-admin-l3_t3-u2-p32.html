Interpersonal skills

12 How this affects you

Most organisations will record some or all telephone calls for monitoring and training purposes. The caller must be made aware that this is happening. As recorded telephone calls will often contain personal information this is covered under the GDPR. Organisations will be required to provide legal justification for the collecting and processing of personal information in this way. They will also need to comply with the other provisions of the GDPR including the rights of

  • access
  • challenge
  • erasure
  • amendment
  • security
  • notification of breaches.

Therefore, any call recording undertaken and retained by a contact centre, be it only for training purposes, are likely to require the permission of the caller, as the catch all phrase ‘calls may be recorded for training purposes’ will no longer be sufficient.
