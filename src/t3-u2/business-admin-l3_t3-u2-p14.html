Interpersonal skills

Active listening

Listening skills have always been valuable and in the workplace are considered to be a key communication skill.

The benefits of developing our ability to effectively listen include:

  • having a better understanding of the problems and challenges colleagues are facing
  • placing a value on what other people say and their input
  • building trust through taking the time to try and understand the motivations of colleagues
  • making better decisions based on more accurate information gained through listening effectively
