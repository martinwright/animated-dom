Interpersonal skills

4 During call tasks

Transferring to another department

When you can’t deal with the request you may need to transfer to others:

  • Ask permission.
  • Give the caller the name of the person you are transferring the call to.
  • Explain why.
  • Give the phone number.
  • Transfer the call.

Taking a message

Get the correct and complete information:

  • Write down information including the date and time.
  • Ask the caller to spell names accurately – people will be impressed you want to get the details correct.
  • What does the caller want? A return call? The caller will call back?
  • When is a good time to call?
  • Ask if you can help.

By ensuring that you have the complete information about the purpose of the call, you can pass on an accurate message, reducing the need for return calls to ask for additional information.

Putting customer on hold

Keep the caller up to date:

  • explain why
  • ask permission
  • put caller on hold
  • thank the caller for holding.
