Interpersonal skills

7 After you have the message

Passing on voicemail

Take ownership and deliver the message within an agreed timeframe to the correct person. Companies use different ways to pass on messages. These can be:

  • email
  • face to face
  • record within a book
  • notelets.

It is vital that you get the message to the person who needs to deal with it.

Deleting messages

You will need to delete messages or a message mailbox will become full. A few things to think about before deleting:

  • Do you have all the details?
  • Will this be followed up?
  • Do you need to keep the message?

With all the information given you need to make sure you are aware of, and fully follow, your organisational procedures and policies when using the telephone system.
