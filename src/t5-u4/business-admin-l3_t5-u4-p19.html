Customer and stakeholder relationship management

2 Clarity over expectations

Linked to the previous point about communication, having absolute clarity over expectations for the partnership is vital, and again should be discussed from the outset. How the partners benefit, and ensuring that they are happy and satisfied with their level of benefit, is key for a successful partnership.

A mismatch in this area that is not understood and addressed will resurface again later when the consequences could be much more severe. This will, of course, require levels of honesty and openness between the potential partners to understand clearly what the expectations are so that the right way forward is established.
