Customer and stakeholder relationship management

6 Planning and organising

Often partnering arrangements are established to deliver a project in the early stages. There may well need to be some formal planning and organising agreed so that accountabilities and responsibilities for certain tasks are clarified and scheduled in for action. Formal planning might include:

  • Gantt charts
  • action plans and work breakdown structure
  • sharing of diaries and schedules for key activities
  • clear allocation of accountabilities and responsibilities for tasks
  • sharing of information about the work to be done.
