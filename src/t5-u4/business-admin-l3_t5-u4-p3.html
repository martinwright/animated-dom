Customer and stakeholder relationship management

The underpinning skills required for effective business relationships

If you consider the skills required for effective business relationships you might identify these skills:

  • effective communication skills which you might have broken down further into areas such as speaking, listening, writing skills and emotional intelligence
  • organisation and planning skills
  • being aware of objectives and expectations
  • building rapport skills and establishing trust
  • setting and managing expectations of different customers, partners and stakeholders
  • being flexible with communication methods and styles
  • networking skills
  • keeping records and maintaining key documentation
  • negotiation and influencing skills.

We are going to look at these skills in more detail throughout your on-programme learning.
