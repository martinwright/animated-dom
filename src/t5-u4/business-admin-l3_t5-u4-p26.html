Customer and stakeholder relationship management

8 Partnership agreements and contracts

During initial negotiations and establishing of a partnership it is key that the legal aspects of the relationship are set out. This will result in a partnership arrangement agreement that will include:

  • the entities party to the partnership agreement
  • the terms of engagement
  • length of the agreement
  • roles and responsibilities under the agreement
  • financial details and arrangements
  • managing confidential information
  • insurance and liabilities
  • how disputes will be resolved
  • changing and terminating the agreement.

It is vital to a successful partnering arrangement that in the early days the organisations involved talk openly and honestly about entering the agreement to ensure it has a strong foundation from the outset.
