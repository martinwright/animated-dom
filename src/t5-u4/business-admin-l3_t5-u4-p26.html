Customer and stakeholder relationship management

9 Communicating with partners

A partner organisation is also a stakeholder; they are also a form of customer. These two dimensions come together to create a type of business relationship that needs a high degree of trust and understanding. Here is a list of the main communication methods likely to be used with partner organisations:

  • face-to-face meetings; one to one or teams
  • online or conference call meetings
  • telephone calls
  • emails
  • document sharing
  • project plans and trackers
  • reports and budgets.
