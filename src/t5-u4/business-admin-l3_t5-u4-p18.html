Customer and stakeholder relationship management

1 Establishing a partner relationship

Successful partner relationships require effort to be made in key areas to ensure they are sustainable over the long term. These include:

Continuously communicate – establishing honest and meaningful communication from the outset and then maintaining it is vital. All of the customer communication skills mentioned earlier apply equally here too. Being able to listen, ask questions, build rapport, manage emotions effectively and gain trust will all build the solid foundation needed to get a partnership off the ground.

Of course this honesty from the outset has another highly valuable function. At this early stage we may, because we have effectively and accurately communicated from the start, discover the partnership is not one for us and decide not to proceed.

Whatever the outcome, the need to communicate effectively means that the partners are likely to:

  • have regular meetings to build rapport and trust
  • meet to discuss expectations and how the parties will benefit
  • introduce key members of each organisation’s team important to the partnership
  • establish the means of and regularity of contact and who will make it
  • create an initial strategy, timetable and plan.
