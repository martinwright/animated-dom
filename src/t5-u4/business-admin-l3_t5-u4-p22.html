Customer and stakeholder relationship management

5 Create a partnership strategy

Of course there will potentially be a lot of detail to discuss as the work between the partners unfolds. However as part of the initial discussions and groundwork, it is worth getting some headline points down that:

  • describe what the partnership is for
  • outline how the parties will work together
  • lay out the shared values between the partnering organisations
  • establish the benefits of the partnership for the partners
  • provide guidance on how the partners will work together.

This does not have to be a long document and could be more of a summary of key points that all the partners agree on, providing guidance on the relationship and how it will be conducted.
