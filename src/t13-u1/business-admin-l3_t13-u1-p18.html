Project lifecycle and roles

5 Team members

Team members can be, for example, employees, agency workers, freelance workers or volunteers.

They might be allocated to, for example:

  • the project full time — eg working in the planning office for the whole project
  • the project part time — eg spending a regular two days a week on the accounts and budgets
  • short-term roles — eg directing cars in a car park for a two-day event.

They will all work within the limits of their authority and refer to the team leader for everyday queries and problems.
