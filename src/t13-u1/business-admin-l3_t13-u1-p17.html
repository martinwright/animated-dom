Project lifecycle and roles

4 Team leaders

As the project is broken down into different components, there may be team leaders in charge of each section. They will answer to the project manager and be responsible for their sub-teams and special areas only, which could include, for example:

  • induction and training — eg of new team members and volunteers
  • a catering team — eg that looks after one of the food court areas in a large venue
  • running one of the bars — eg one of 10 bars in the large venue
  • a customer service team — eg dealing with customer queries at information points
  • a media team — that looks after press, TV and radio coverage.
