Level 3 Business Administrator | Project lifecycle and roles

Introduction

In the workplace, people perform a wide range of tasks. Some of these will be routine and repetitive tasks that have to be done every day or every week. In the home, these tasks would be regular tasks like washing up, laundry or vacuuming the floor.

At work, other tasks will be part of planned projects and will not be routine. The equivalent at home would be moving house or decorating a room – one-off projects that require a great deal of planning and preparation.

In this section, we are going to look at:

  • an example of a work-based project
  • the keys stages in the lifecycle of a project
  • roles within the project team.
