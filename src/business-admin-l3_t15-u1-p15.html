5 Corporate identity

This is where organisations have logos, branding and a mission statement. Organisations become instantly recognisable by their logos.

Also, when producing documents, organisations sometimes use what is a called a 'house style'.

House style means all documents that are produced have the same layout/style and maintain the organisation's professional image.

House style factors include:

  • font style and colour
  • size of font
  • style and position of headings
  • spacing
  • justification of the text
  • size of the margins.

Many of the documents produced are printed onto letter headed paper.
