Organising meetings and events

4 PDCA cycles

This section aims to develop the knowledge and skills you need to organise meetings and events. Businesses organise many types of events depending on the organisation and its sector. There are several different types of events and ways that will help you organise yourself and resources. When working through this section you will learn about:

  • different types of meetings
  • preparing for a meeting
  • meeting procedures
  • following up on meetings
  • how to chair a meeting
  • the characteristics, requirements and purposes of different types of events
  • information sources needed to organise an event
  • types of information needed before, during and after an event.
