Motivate and influence

Key terms

  • Motivation – the internal and external factors that inspire desire and energy in people to be continually interested and committed to a job, role or subject, or to make an effort to attain a goal.
  • Motivating factors – the drivers of human behaviour related to the essential nature of their work, but not necessarily to the surrounding circumstances or environment. These drivers may include achievement, advancement, autonomy, personal growth, recognition, responsibility and the work itself.
  • Employee engagement – the connection an employee feels towards the organisation, which tends to influence their behaviours and level of effort.
