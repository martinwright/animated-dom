Motivate and influence

5 Influence

Whatever your role or responsibilities, it is important to be able to achieve influence in the workplace. The ability to influence teams helps them work more effectively together. In supervisory positions, influence is about gaining respect and appreciation. In meetings it is important to have influence so that your ideas and experiences are heard and acknowledged.
