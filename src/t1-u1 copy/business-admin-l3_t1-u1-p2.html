Professionalism

Key terms

  • Role model
    a person who serves as an example of the values, attitudes and behaviours associated with a role. A person who distinguishes themselves in such a way that others admire and want to emulate them.
  • Professional behaviour
    a series of actions considered acceptable in the workplace, dictated by concepts like courtesy, civility and good taste.
  • Desired professional image
    what a person wants their customers, superiors, subordinates and colleagues to say about them when they’re not in the room.
  • Perceived professional image
    what a person thinks their customers, superiors, subordinates and colleagues say about them when they’re not in the room.
