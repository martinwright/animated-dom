Coaching and mentoring

Key terms

  • Coaching — a form of development in which a person supports a learner in achieving a specific personal or professional goal by providing training and guidance.
  • Mentoring — supporting and encouraging people to manage their own learning in order that they may maximise their potential, develop their skills, improve their performance and become the person they want to be.
  • Best practice — commercial or professional procedures that are accepted or prescribed as being correct or most effective.
