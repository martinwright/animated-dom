Coaching and mentoring

Introduction

In this section, you will learn about the differences between coaching and mentoring and how to apply coaching and mentoring skills in your role.

Mentoring is the long-term passing on of support, guidance and advice.

Coaching is a two-way process where the team member takes an active part and the coach acts as a facilitator. It focuses on development and is usually done on a one-to-one basis, although it can be used for small groups or teams.
