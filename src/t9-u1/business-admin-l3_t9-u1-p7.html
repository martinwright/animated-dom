Change in the workplace

3 Conflict within the team

Conflict between team members can occur if change is not managed well â€“ eg due to stress and uncertainty following changes in workloads; as a result of changes to status, tasks or responsibilities; due to a lack of skills, knowledge or experience of new working practices or procedures; due to lack of meaningful information.

Some team members may enjoy the challenge of change and embrace the difficulties as learning and development opportunities. On the other hand, some team members will fear change and be deeply unsettled by it. This can cause conflict for a while, until everyone finds their new place and settles into new ways of working.
