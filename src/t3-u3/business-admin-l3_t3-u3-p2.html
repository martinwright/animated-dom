Digital and social media

Key terms

  • Social media
    – websites and computer programs that allow people to communicate and share information on the Internet using a computer or mobile phone.
  • Code of conduct
    – a set of rules about how to behave and do business with other people.
  • Content curation
    – the act of discovering, gathering and presenting digital content about specific subject matters.
  • Videography
    – the process or art of making video films.
  • Analytics
    – the discovery, interpretation and communication of meaningful patterns in data.
