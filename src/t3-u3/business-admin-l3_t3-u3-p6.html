Digital and social media

3 Types of social media

Social media is a broad term for internet-based tools used to help people make contacts, keep in touch with family, friends and colleagues and interact on a wide range of digital devices.

Hold

Allows you to place a current call on hold. You can then answer another ringing telephone, make an outgoing call, or retrieve a waiting call.

Call transfer

Allows you to transfer a call to another telephone.

Conference call

You can create conference calls with this feature.

Mute

Allows you to hear what's being said on the phone, but they cannot hear you.

Speaker

You can put the caller on loud speaker so others can hear.

Company directory

You can have access to all company staff members via their number using the directory.

Call waiting

Allows a user to hear a tone when they are on the line to notify them that another caller is trying to reach them. Users have the option of answering the second call or allowing it to go unanswered.

Voicemail

You can set up a personal message instructing callers on how to leave you a message.
