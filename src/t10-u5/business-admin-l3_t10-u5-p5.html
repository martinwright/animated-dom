Supply chains

2 Flows in a supply chain

The three flows in a supply chain involve materials, information and capital.

Downstream:

  • materials, eg products and parts
  • information, eg capacity, delivery logistics schedules
  • finance, eg invoices, credit terms, prices.

Upstream:

  • materials, eg returns, servicing and maintenance
  • information, eg orders, retail data
  • finance, eg payments.
