Supply chains

Supply chains and supply chain management

1 Supply chains

The network of businesses involved in a supply chain include:

  • materials flow from suppliers – this may be businesses involved in the extraction of raw materials who then undertake ‘upstream’ supply to manufacturers
  • transformation of materials into semi-finished or finished products
  • distribution of products to customers – this may be a retail chain which then undertakes ‘downstream’ distribution to the ultimate user of the product.
