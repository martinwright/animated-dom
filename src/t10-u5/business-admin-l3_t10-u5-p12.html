Supply chains

9 Technology

Technology is increasingly used to improve supply chain management.

  • Forecasting and planning software – eg material and manufacturing requirements, time phased scheduling.
  • Internet communication tools – eg real-time communication on orders, returns, inventory, financial transactions – reduces the bullwhip effect by displaying requirements throughout the chain.
  • Integrated supply chains – eg large retailers and manufacturers integrate supply chains so that they act like a single company of upstream and downstream partners.
