Supply chains

5 Order fulfilment

There are a number of steps to ensure effective order fulfilment:

  1. payment clearance
  2. in-stock availability
  3. packaging and shipment
  4. insurance
  5. production
  6. plant services
  7. purchasing and warehousing
  8. forecasting of demand
  9. finance – accounting and billing
  10. customer relationship management
  11. returns – reverse logistics.
