Key terms

  • Autonomously
    with the freedom to act independently.
  • SWOT analysis
    identifying strengths, weaknesses, opportunities and threats.
  • Inefficient
    not making the best use of time or resources.
  • Ineffective
    not producing any significant or desired effect.
  • Process
    a set or series of actions directed to some end.
  • Procedure
    an established way of doing something.
