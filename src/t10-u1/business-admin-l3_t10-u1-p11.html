3 Partnerships

Partnerships are often set up if a small number of people are involved (up to about 20) and limited liability is not necessary. To set up a partnership there are various processes that must be followed:

  • A nominated person is chosen and is responsible for managing the partnership’s tax returns and keeping business records.
  • Partners must be registered for tax self-assessment with HMRC.
  • Official paperwork includes all the partner’s names as well as the business name.
