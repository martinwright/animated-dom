The private sector

1 Private sector organisations

This is the part of the economy that is not state controlled, and is run by individuals and companies for profit. The private sector encompasses all for-profit businesses that are not owned or operated by the government. The private sector employs most people in free-market economies. There are five different types of business in this sector:

  • sole trader
  • partnership
  • private limited company
  • public limited company
  • charity.
