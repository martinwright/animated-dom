Personal development planning

Key stages in development planning

There are several stages to go through to plan personal career development and focus on the three questions above. You need to:

  • analyse current skills, knowledge and experience – to identify skills gaps and where you are now
  • identify development needs and set objectives – to focus on where you need to be
  • identify your learning styles
  • arrange resources and support mechanisms to meet the objectives – the basis of how you are going to achieve your goals
  • monitor and review progress and overcome barriers to learning – to make sure you are still going the right way.
