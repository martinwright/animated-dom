Personal development planning

Key terms

  • Initiative – the ability to use your judgement to make decisions and do things without needing to be told what to do.
  • Resilience – the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties.
  • Training needs analysis – the process of identifying gaps in training and related training needs.
  • Personal development plan – an action plan based on awareness, values, reflection, goal-setting and planning for personal development within the context of a career, education or a relationship, or for self-improvement.
  • Appraisal 360 report – a type of employee performance review in which subordinates, co-workers and managers all rate the employee.
