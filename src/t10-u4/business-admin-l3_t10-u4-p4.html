Understand the market

Issues and trends

Understanding the current issues and trends in your sector enables the forecasting of demand for products and services and the developments which might need to take place to ensure that your business continues to succeed and grow.

For example, current issues and trends in the hospitality sector include:

  • sustainability – demand for reduced packaging and plastic
  • fairtrade products
  • provenance and food miles
  • healthier lifestyles – more international cuisine, less people ordering starters and desserts, more people ordering food to share
  • less disposable income
  • political instability
  • fear of food allergies/intolerances
  • ethics – increased demand for organic or vegan food
  • value.

The issues and trends will be different in your sector but it is important to research them and ensure that you keep up to date. Businesses which innovate stay 'ahead of the curve' in retaining customers, and increasing demand for their products and services.
