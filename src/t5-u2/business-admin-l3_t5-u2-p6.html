Cross-team working

2 Benefits of cross-team working

Benefits of cross-team working include, for example:

  • having a shared purpose and capacity to achieve complex and difficult objectives – that could not be done by one team on their own
  • opportunities to discuss shared goals – and how to achieve objectives together
  • improved relationships between different teams – from enhanced understanding of each team’s issues and expectations
  • diverse and flexible team members – who can perform a variety of tasks, work on more than one team and enhance their career development potential
  • good internal communication – from establishing joint procedures and keeping each other informed at every stage
  • consistent standards of quality and output – from collaboration about standards and how to achieve excellence
  • a broader team spirit – bringing different teams together and reducing friction between them.
