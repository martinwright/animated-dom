Level 3 Business Administrator | Accuracy and proofing

Introduction

It is important to check your work for spelling, punctuation and grammar. We all make mistakes when we are writing and it is important that you check your work for these mistakes. If you are writing on a computer, you could use a spellchecker (although the spellchecker will only pick up words that are not spelt correctly); if you are writing by hand, you could use a dictionary.
